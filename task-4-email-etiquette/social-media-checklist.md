## Responsible Social Media Use Checklist (College Students)
### Do’s
- **Think before posting** – Ensure your content is respectful and appropriate.  
- **Protect your privacy** – Use strong passwords and adjust privacy settings.  
- **Verify information** – Check facts before sharing any news or content.  
- **Maintain a positive digital footprint** – Share meaningful and constructive posts.  
- **Use social media productively** – Follow educational pages and build your network.  
---
### Don’ts
- **Don’t share personal or sensitive information** – Avoid posting your address, phone number, or passwords.  
- **Don’t engage in cyberbullying or hate speech** – Always communicate respectfully.  
- **Don’t spread misinformation** – Avoid forwarding unverified news or rumors.  
- **Don’t overshare** – Posting excessively can affect your image and privacy.  
- **Don’t ignore time management** – Avoid wasting excessive time scrolling.

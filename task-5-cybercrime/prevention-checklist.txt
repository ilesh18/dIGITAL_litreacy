Stay Safe Online — Checklist for College Students (India)

⁜ Prevention Tips:
-Create complex and unique passwords for all accounts.
-Activate 2FA for your email, social media, and banking apps.
-Never give your OTP or passwords to anyone, no matter what they claim.
-Always check the authenticity of the link before clicking on it.
-Never give your personal information online, such as your phone number, address, or id card details.
-Always log out of shared devices, such as college labs or cyber cafes.
-Never use public Wi-Fi for your online banking or any important account log-ins.

⁜ Financial / UPI Safety Tips:
-Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money. You only need it when sending the money.
-Always double-check the payment requests. Scammers may ask for “collect requests” instead of sending the money.
-Never download any unknown apps or screen-sharing apps suggested by unknown people.
-Always check the authenticity of the QR code before scanning it. Scammers may replace the original QR code with the fake one.

##  Project Overview
This project focuses on building awareness about **digital literacy, online safety, and responsible internet usage** among college students. It covers practical aspects such as cybercrime awareness, safe social media practices, and digital communication skills.

The project includes creating a quiz, writing case studies, designing checklists, and understanding real-world digital risks like UPI fraud.

---

##  Module Summaries

### 1. Digital Platforms & Usage
Explored platforms like GitHub, Kaggle, and LinkedIn and how they help in skill-building, networking, and showcasing projects.

### 2. Google Form Quiz
Created a **Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz** using Google Forms with multiple question types to test basic online safety knowledge.

### 3. Professional Communication
Drafted formal emails for academic and professional purposes, improving communication skills.

### 4. Social Media Responsibility
Developed a checklist of Do’s and Don’ts for safe and responsible social media usage.

### 5. Cybercrime Case Study
Studied **UPI/Online Payment Fraud**, including how it happens, who is targeted, and its consequences.

### 6. Online Safety Checklist
Designed a **“Stay Safe Online” checklist** with actionable tips and reporting channels.

### 7. Reflection & Learning
Reflected on key learnings, including the importance of awareness, caution, and verification in digital environments.
